CHI 2026 will be held at the Centre de Convencions Internacional de Barcelona.
See their official website for instructions how to get there: <https://ccib.es/en/barcelona/#ccib_arribar>

The workshop is in **P1 - Room 127** (3. Convention Centre - P1). See the [CHI 2026 programme map](https://programs.sigchi.org/chi/2026/maps/10475?roomName=P1%20-%20Room%20127) for the room location. For a sense of the layout, a [360° panorama of a similar room](https://ccib.es/venuetour/index.html?panorama=118) is available on the CCIB website.

The room has a seating capacity of 75. We are expecting 15–20 participants. The workshop is invite-only. The room is equipped with a projector, screen, microphones, and speakers.
