CHI 2026 was held at the [Centre de Convencions Internacional de Barcelona](https://ccib.es/en/barcelona/).

The workshop was held in **P1 - Room 127** (3. Convention Centre - P1). See the [CHI 2026 programme map](https://programs.sigchi.org/chi/2026/maps/10475?roomName=P1%20-%20Room%20127) for the room location. A [360° panorama of a similar room](https://ccib.es/venuetour/index.html?panorama=118) is available on the CCIB website.

The room had a seating capacity of 75 and was equipped with a projector, screen, microphones, and speakers. We had around 20 participants attend the workshop.
